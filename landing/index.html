A RetroVerse Studios game · free & open source

Swipe to shape the fate of entire realities.

SwipeVerse is a card-based survival strategy game. Every card is a dilemma; every swipe nudges Power, Wealth, People and Knowledge. Let any of them hit zero — or a hundred — and your reign is over.

▶  Play free in your browser View on GitHub

No account, no download, works offline. Install it as an app from the game menu (Chrome/Android) or via Share → Add to Home Screen (iPhone).

How it plays

  1. Pick a reality — each reskins the four stats into its own world.
  2. Swipe left or right — every dilemma has two answers, and both cost you something.
  3. Watch the meters — hit 0 or 100 on any stat and it's game over.
  4. Reach the final card — survive 20 nodes of branching story to win the run.
